About Gopher Films

Founded 2014, Team Gopher saw the first Gopher Films cinema open within the Trinity shopping center. It is a place where films can be experienced with comfort and enjoyment. Our excellent customer service makes us stand out from our competitors as the best location for an enjoyable cinema experience.


We offer a wide range of confectionary and drinks options for our customers to ensure that the viewing experience is the best we can provide. Our VIP lounge provides a private bar and free unlimited confectionary and soft drinks. The lounge is the perfect place to get the most out of your cinema experience.
